About Fish The.Box

Fish The.Box was created by a team of developers who share a passion for both technology and fishing. The idea began as a simple project for keeping fishing trip information organized and easy to find.

As anglers ourselves, we often found it difficult to manage trip details, track upcoming outings, and check weather conditions before heading to the water.

Fish The.Box helps anglers plan trips, stay informed, and keep track of their fishing activities.
